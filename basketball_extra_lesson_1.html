üìñ KEY VOCABULARY

Subtract:

Take away some objects from a group

Example: 7 basketballs - 3 basketballs = 4 basketballs left

Minus:

Another word for subtract (shown with the - sign)

Example: 8 - 2 means "8 minus 2"

Difference:

The answer when you subtract

Example: In 6 - 4 = 2, the difference is 2
